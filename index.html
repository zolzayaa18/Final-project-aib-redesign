The Challenge

Why This Redesign Matters

Irish banking customers increasingly expect the personalised experience of apps like Revolut, while demanding the transparency and trustworthiness that traditional banks should provide — but often don't.

  • Reactive, Not Proactive

    Current apps tell you what happened — not what's about to happen with your money.

  • Black Box AI

    70% want personalisation, but only 23% trust AI recommendations without explanation.

  • Unclear Communication

    Confusing notifications, unexplained decisions, and no visibility into how recommendations are made.
